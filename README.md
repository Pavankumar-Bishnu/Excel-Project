## Data Overviwe                     :
This dataset ( Global Super Store Data ) includes sales transactions recorded between April **2018 and October 2020**. It captures details related to pricing, discounts, costs, and profitability across various market segments.

- Date range (from Date column): 2018-01-01 to 2020-12-31 (years present: 2018, 2019, 2020)
- Rows                               : 1,096
- Columns                            : 15
- Segment (top 5)                    : Government (387), Small Business (198), Channel Partners (175), Enterprise (171), Midmarket (165).
- Country (top 5)                    : United States of America (232), Mexico (223), Germany (216), Canada (215), France (210).
- Discount Band (top counts)         : Medium (423), High (355), Low (247), None (71).
- Gross Sales monthly totals (sample): many months are in millions; the highest monthly Gross Sales observed was 17,624,008.0 on 2018-10-31.
- Units Sold and price columns (Manufacturing Price, Sale Price) present sensible numeric ranges.
- Total Units Sold                   : 1,764,873
- Total Gross Sales                  : 202,598,711.50
- Total Net Sales                    : 187,027,933.60
- Total Profit                       : 25,437,030.60
